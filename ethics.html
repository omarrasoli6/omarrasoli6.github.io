IT-Related Ethics

1. Guidelines for Developers

  • Write secure and maintainable code.
  • Respect user privacy and confidentiality.
  • Avoid conflicts of interest in software design.
  • Follow proper licensing and copyright practices.

2. Ethical Responsibilities for Data Scientists

  • Be transparent about data collection and use.
  • Minimize bias in algorithms and datasets.
  • Obtain proper consent for using data.
  • Consider social and legal implications of analysis.
