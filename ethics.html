IT-Related Ethics

1. Guidelines for Developers

  • Write clean, maintainable, and secure code.
  • Respect user privacy and handle data responsibly.
  • Disclose conflicts of interest when developing software.
  • Follow open-source licensing and intellectual property laws.

2. Ethical Considerations for Data Scientists

  • Ensure transparency in data collection and usage.
  • Avoid bias in algorithms and datasets.
  • Respect consent and data ownership.
  • Understand the social impacts of predictive models.
